About Our Cooperative

Working together for sustainable agriculture and prosperous farming communities

Our Journey Since 1995

Established in 1995, The Kamatalav Ganotiya Kheti Sahakari Mandali has been at the forefront of agricultural development in our region. What began as a small group of visionary farmers has grown into a robust cooperative with over 850 members.

Our mission is to empower farmers through collective strength, knowledge sharing, and access to modern agricultural resources. We operate on principles of mutual aid, democratic control, and economic participation.

We specialize in supporting farmers with:

  • Access to quality seeds and organic fertilizers
  • Modern irrigation techniques and water conservation
  • Market linkages for better price realization
  • Training in sustainable farming practices
  • Financial support and crop insurance

Modern Farming Techniques

Promoting sustainable and efficient agricultural practices

Precision Agriculture

Using technology to optimize field-level management:

  • GPS-guided equipment for accurate planting
  • Soil sensors for moisture and nutrient monitoring
  • Drone-based field analysis
  • Variable rate technology for inputs

Organic Farming

Chemical-free cultivation methods we promote:

  • Natural pest control with neem and other biopesticides
  • Composting and vermicompost production
  • Crop rotation and polyculture systems
  • Green manure and cover cropping

Water Conservation

Efficient water management techniques:

  • Drip irrigation systems
  • Rainwater harvesting
  • Soil moisture conservation methods
  • Water-efficient crop selection
  • Mulching techniques
